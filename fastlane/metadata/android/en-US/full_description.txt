Notif Logs is a local Android notification history manager by Arif.

It records Android notifications using the Notification Listener Service and stores the history locally on the user's device.

Features:
- Local notification history
- Search notification records
- Group notifications by app name and notification title
- Whitelist and blacklist apps
- Dark mode support
- Clear notification history management
- No ads
- No tracking
- No external server

Privacy:
Notif Logs stores notification data locally on the device. The app does not upload, share, track, or send notification data to any third-party service.
